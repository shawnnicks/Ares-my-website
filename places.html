Pares is a beloved Filipino comfort food consisting of tender, slow-cooked beef served with a flavorful sweet-savory broth. Famous for its Goto, Pares Rice, and Butchay Pares, it’s a hearty and satisfying dish that brings together rich flavors perfect for any meal. Often paired with garlic rice and a side of hot broth, it’s a true Filipino classic. The main branch is located near Gaisano Grand Mall.
